== Why do I get log messages on my console?

Either add loglevel=3 or quiet to your kernel parameters.

== How to power off or reboot machine?

'systemctl poweroff' or 'systemctl reboot'

== How to get current boot log

----
journactl -b
----

Only errors

----
journalctl -b -p err
----
