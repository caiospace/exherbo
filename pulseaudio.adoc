== alsamixer - Permission denied

When the PulseAudio ALSA plugins are active, you must explicitly specify your hardware device in alsamixer (marked in
blue above), otherwise it will open the PulseAudio mixer.
My main soundcard

----
alsamixer -Dhw
----

My usb Microphone

----
alsamixer -Dhw:1
----
